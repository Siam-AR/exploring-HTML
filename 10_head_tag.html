Understanding the HTML Head Tag

The <head> section of an HTML document contains metadata, links to stylesheets, scripts, and other information that does not appear directly on the webpage. Proper use of meta tags and title improves SEO and accessibility. For example, the viewport meta tag ensures responsive design on mobile devices.
